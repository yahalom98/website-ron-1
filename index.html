My Project

Overview

The project I am interested to present to you is something that can help a vast range of industries, companies, and individuals around the globe but you will explore more in-depth about the idea in the upcoming sections. Have you ever heard about drones? The small flying machines with the cameras that people control to take photos? I may guess the answer is yes but let me get things more interesting than that insight... Something more intuitive, calculative, fast and can help to enhance our lives to become better. I am talking about fully transformed AI-drone which can do more tasks and beat most of the current performances of today’s technologies.

Motivation

In my opinion, this project can revolutionize many sectors like Mining, Farming, Manufacturing, Healthcare and many more with the help of advanced technology with little to no help from humans. Imagine a world that robots or in this case self calculative drones will help us with our daily jobs and even beyond. Whether it is carrying groceries, helping us with deliveries, logistics, security and even saving someone’s lives, everything can be done with a tool that can be enhanced and progressed mostly on its own. To understand the actual need in fully AI drones and drones in general, we need to look at the market value prediction. From 2016 to 2020 the market value is estimated by 100BN dollars only from the commercial and civil government sectors: 70BN dollars only from military and defense, 17BN dollars from consumer drone market and 13BN dollars from businesses and civil governments, All this data information is according to Goldman Sachs insight report. We can understand this industry has a lot of potential with helping in a vast range of important sectors but not only that. Let’s also look at the enhancement we can achieve with companies that can not only use drones and something that save time and money but also as something that can open the growth of more job openings in the future.

Description

The Internet of Things, or in other words IOT, has revolutionized our era with advanced technology capabilities, commercial usage, and many more opportunities to fulfill in various sectors. My project is something that is very familiar in the ‘IoT family’ and has been used for several years for different types of tasks but now it is going to be changed and enhanced. I am going to explain how a sophisticated drone with a little touch of existing tools can magnify our world. First, before we are going to dig deep into the project let’s define several key elements that are important to understand the project and the actual need for it. The first thing is A.I or in other words Artificial Intelligence which is the ability to diagnose and perform intellectual tasks and reach a level of self-taught machine that will perform like the mind of humans. The second thing we need to define is what a drone is.
According to Space.com, “Drone refers to an unpiloted aircraft or spacecraft." (Elizabeth Howell,What Is A Drone? Space.com, October 03, 2018)

Or in technological terms, a drone is a flying robot that can fly and be controlled autonomously using controlling software, GPS and sensors. So you are asking, how a flying machine that already exists on the market can help us to exceed a better quality of life quality, financial aid, save tons of time and resources which is can be crucial in the long term vision. My project will be doing several things, to explain the features of the product in-depth I must explain how it can help each sector individually.

Farming:

In the farming sector we face many problems and just to name the main which are yield waste and manpower The AI drone can easily learn on its own and in this case, it can learn map and image patterns that can help us in the first problem, it can detect if the fruit or vegetable is going to get rotten or dry and immediately supply essential care. Furthermore, it can help us to manage and measure spraying and carrying faster reliability than humans which saves time and resources which is indicated for solving the second problem.

Mining:

In the mining sector, we can face complicated tasks that humans can execute but that involves risking their lives. To solve this, we specified that AI drones can analyze and learn according to patterns with simple commands and manage almost like humans. While entering hazard zones and unreachable places a drone can be a perfect solution. It can be sent out, supply necessary tools, food, heavy machinery, supplies and keeping the safety of the workers. Furthermore, drones can also save time with data collection. Mapping and constructing safety plans for undefined zones can be a really difficult task to perform. Smart drones can collect data and manipulate or translate large quantities or areas faster and reliable than humans which can save tremendous amounts of money and essential time.

Healthcare:

In my opinion, one of the most important sectors that drones can assist in healthcare. It can help with medication and tools delivery, instead of relying on nurses and save manpower, crucial time and it is efficient to use in the long term. Also, AI drones can analyze areas and people at risk where there is no medical assistance and provide the needed medication and help which means it can act as a nurse when help is needed.

Military:

In the military sector, we face problems like security and saving lives and we encounter problems that an AI drone can easily manage. First, the drone is only controlled by remote control or by a chip that controls its moves which can idealize our need for security and search & rescue. In combat or a dangerous situation, AI drones can detect and enhance rescue efforts by searching people and picking them up, just like a helicopter but small, much efficient and very cheap. Also, drones can easily retrieve phenomenal information and status about surveillance and advanced patterns of areas that can help with supervision and controlling necessary areas without risking lives with surveillance.

Tools and Technologies:

Software: In order to create the user interface for controlling the basic elements in the drone, connect the starting building and initiate the connectivity between the hardware to the code we can use Cyclon.js or Johnny-Five which are JavaScript framework that supports Robots and IoT. It is easy to learn, it’s free and reliable.
Hardware: for the hardware, we need something that will act fast, reliable and cheap. For that, I would use Arduino. An open-source for hardware and program functionality. In addition, we need different kinds of physical drones for different tasks.

Skills Required:

For this extremely exciting mission but also difficult to solve we need special skills. This task, I need people who can think outside the box with a passion for IoT and specifically drones. The project will be developed using NodeJS, JavaScript, React, TensorFlow, Docker, AWS, Leaflet.js, and Johnny-Five. The project will include developing a user-friendly interface for each different drone with its own needs and algorithm for Artificial Intelligence.
